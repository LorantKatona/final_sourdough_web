Sourdough is a bread made from the natural occurring yeast and bacteria in flour. In traditional sourdough recipes, you’ll find three ingredients:

  • sourdough starter (which consists of flour and water)
  • salt
  • and flour
These three ingredients are enough to prepare a tasty and traditional bread. There is no yeast, no milk, no oils and no sweeteners. It’s about as natural as you get when it comes to bread.
In addition to having a very distinctive and delicious taste, sourdough bread has a lot of helath benefits. Fist of all supports gut health. The fermentation process and higher fibre content makes sourdough a useful option for those with blood sugar management issues, furthermore offer additional benefits against developing heart diseas. Not at least it appears to be more nutritious owing to the long tofermentation process which helps phytic acid to break down enabling us to access the grain's nutrients more readily.
