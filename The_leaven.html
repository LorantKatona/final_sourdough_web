Unlike yeast based doughs most doughs which are based on leaven require lots of proving and rising time. However, different bakery products require different dough. The hydration of the doughs may vary from 50% to 85%. Usually, the dough should be soft and smooth.It should not leave a residue on your hands, but should not be super stiff either. If you're making bread by hand (just like me 😃), there are three steps essential to success. After you mix the starter, the flour and the water let the dough to rest about 30 minutes. No, I did not forget to add the salt, intentionally I did not put it yet into the dough because without salt the microorganisms will reproduce faster. During this resting time the flour will get hydrated and the kneading will be easier.
